## SECTION 3: PROBLEM STATEMENT

### The Problem

Students face three critical challenges:

**1. Information Overload**
The average university course requires reading 500+ pages per semester. Students struggle to identify what's important, leading to inefficient studying and poor retention.

**2. Passive Learning Inefficiency**
85% of students rely on passive techniques (re-reading, highlighting) despite research showing these methods have minimal impact on long-term retention. Active learning techniques are 2-3x more effective but require tools most students don't have.

**3. Fragmented Tools**
Students juggle 5-7 different apps (Quizlet, Notion, ChatGPT, etc.) to study effectively. This creates friction, wastes time, and prevents integrated learning insights.
