## üé¨ VEO 3 BEST PRACTICES

### Prompt Engineering Tips
1. **Be Specific About Style**: Reference real cinematographers, camera models, or visual styles (e.g., "Canon C300 look", "Apple commercial aesthetic")
2. **Describe Lighting**: Always specify lighting direction, color temperature, quality
3. **Camera Movement**: Explicitly state "static", "dolly", "push-in", "orbit", etc.
4. **Duration Precision**: Veo 3 performs best with 6-10 second segments
5. **Avoid Complex Physics**: Stick to smooth, simple motions rather than complex simulations
6. **UI Elements**: Describe text, buttons, colors clearly - Veo 3 can generate readable text

### What Veo 3 Excels At
- ‚úÖ Smooth camera movements (dolly, orbit, push-in)
- ‚úÖ Realistic human subjects and expressions
- ‚úÖ UI/UX interface animations
- ‚úÖ Lighting and atmosphere
- ‚úÖ Particle effects and data visualizations
- ‚úÖ Logo animations and typography
- ‚úÖ Product shots with depth of field

### What to Avoid
- ‚ùå Complex character dialogue (lip-sync issues)
- ‚ùå Fast action sequences with physics
- ‚ùå More than 2-3 subjects in frame
- ‚ùå Long duration clips (>10s can drift from prompt)
- ‚ùå Extreme camera angles or fisheye effects
- ‚ùå Hand-held shaky cam (specify "smooth gimbal" if needed)

### Generation Strategy
1. **Generate Each Scene Separately**: Create 6-10 second clips individually
2. **A/B Test**: Generate 2-3 variations per scene and pick the best
3. **Consistency**: Use same style keywords across all scenes
4. **Iterate**: Regenerate with refined prompts if first attempt doesn't match
5. **Post-Production**: Combine clips in editing software (Adobe Premiere, Final Cut Pro, DaVinci Resolve)

## üìù SAMPLE VEO 3 GENERATION COMMAND

Using Google AI Studio or Vertex AI:

```python
import google.generativeai as genai

genai.configure(api_key="YOUR_API_KEY")

model = genai.GenerativeModel('veo-3')

prompt = """
Cinematic close-up shot of a stressed college student sitting at a cluttered desk
in a modern apartment, surrounded by tall stacks of textbooks, scattered papers,
and open PDF files on laptop screen. Dim warm lighting with desk lamp, late evening
atmosphere. Student rubbing tired eyes, looking overwhelmed. Camera slowly pushes
in on face, shallow depth of field. Photorealistic, documentary style, natural
skin tones, 4K quality, Canon C300 look.
"""

response = model.generate_video(
    prompt=prompt,
    duration=8,  # seconds
    aspect_ratio="16:9",
    resolution="1080p",
    camera_motion="push-in",
    num_variants=3  # Generate 3 options to choose from
)

# Download best variant
response.download("scene_01_problem.mp4")
```
